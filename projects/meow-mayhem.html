Unity party game

Meow Mayhem

A 2-4 player party game where warehouse cats throw boxes, collect powerups, and survive level-specific chaos.

I co-created Meow Mayhem as part of LOOPHOLE GAMES with Niko Mlynarčík and Krištof Teplan. The game focuses on fast local multiplayer rounds, readable arenas, changing rules, power-ups and compact comedic interactions. There are multiple game modes like PvP, conveyor delivery, have the least boxes on your half and reach the top of the tower.

Project focus

Each level changes the way players think about movement, pickups, box throwing, and risk. The goal was to keep the game instantly understandable while giving each arena its own identity.
