Unity party game

Meow Mayhem

A 2-4 player party game where warehouse cats throw boxes, collect powerups, and survive level-specific chaos.

I co-created Meow Mayhem as part of LOOPHOLE GAMES with Niko Mlynarčík and Krištof Teplan. The game focuses on fast local multiplayer rounds, readable arenas, changing rules, and compact comedic interactions.

Project focus

Each level changes the way players think about movement, pickups, box throwing, and risk. The goal was to keep the game instantly understandable while giving each arena its own identity.

Contributions

  • Collaborated on game design and multiplayer-feeling level concepts.
  • Worked within a Unity production pipeline.
  • Helped shape a party-game tone built around simple rules and escalating disruption.
